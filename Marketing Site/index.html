Features

Everything Your ER Needs

From AI-powered triage to real-time bed management, we've built every tool your emergency department needs to deliver faster, better care.

AI-Powered Triage

Automatic urgency classification using Llama 3.3 AI. Categorizes patients as Immediate, Urgent, Less Urgent, or Non-Urgent in seconds.

Real-Time Bed Management

Visual bed grid with live availability, assignments, and cleaning status. Optimize bed turnover and reduce patient wait times.

Multi-Hospital Platform

Fully multi-tenant architecture with isolated data per hospital. Manage an entire hospital chain from a single platform.

Smart Alerts

Critical patient alerts and bed availability notifications sent to the right staff in real time. Never miss a critical event.

Dashboard Analytics

Real-time ER statistics, patient flow analysis, and department performance metrics at a glance. Data-driven decisions made easy.

Role-Based Access

Granular permissions for nurses, doctors, and admins. Each role sees exactly what they need, nothing more.

Police Case Tracking

Integrated medico-legal case management. Track FIR numbers, investigating officers, and case status alongside patient records.

Digital Prescriptions

Create and manage prescriptions digitally with templates. Reduce errors and improve pharmacy coordination.

OPD Management

Outpatient department scheduling and tracking. Manage appointments, specialties, and patient queues efficiently.

SaaS Ready

Built-in subscription billing, hospital onboarding, and platform administration. Deploy for one hospital or a hundred.
